## 1. Traffic & Acquisition KPIs

### 1.1 Monthly Unique Visitors

| Period | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Month 3 | 5,000 | Initial organic traction from 50+ indexed pages; SEO takes 3--6 months for new domains (industry standard) |
| Month 6 | 15,000 | SEO compounding; 150+ indexed pages; social media referrals |
| Month 12 | 50,000 | Established domain authority; 200+ indexed pages; backlink profile maturing |

**Benchmark context:** mybest achieves 30M+ monthly users with thousands of articles and years of SEO history. A new entrant targeting a niche (English-language Japan products) can realistically capture 50K in Year 1 based on long-tail keyword opportunities with low competition.

### 1.2 Organic Search Traffic Share

| Period | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Month 3 | 40% of total traffic | Early phase relies on social + direct; organic building |
| Month 6 | 55% of total traffic | SEO traction begins compounding |
| Month 12 | 65% of total traffic | Organic becomes primary channel |

**Benchmark:** Content-driven affiliate sites typically derive 60--70% of traffic from organic search (source: AuthorityHacker affiliate marketing benchmarks).

### 1.3 Google Indexed Pages

| Period | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Month 3 | 75 pages |
| Month 6 | 200 pages |
| Month 12 | 400 pages |

## 2. Engagement KPIs

### 2.1 Average Session Duration

| Period | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Month 3 | > 2:00 min | Baseline with limited content; users exploring |
| Month 6 | > 3:00 min | Netflix-style browsing encourages exploration; more content available |
| Month 12 | > 3:30 min | Deep category coverage encourages multi-page sessions |

**Benchmark:** The average session duration for e-commerce and retail sites is 2:03 minutes; comparison/review sites typically achieve 3--5 minutes due to research intent (source: Contentsquare Digital Experience Benchmarks).

### 2.2 Bounce Rate

| Period | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Month 3 | < 55% | New site, limited content, acceptable for launch |
| Month 6 | < 45% | Internal linking and browse UX reduces bounces |
| Month 12 | < 40% | Strong content depth and Netflix-style discovery retain users |

**Benchmark:** Average bounce rate for content websites is 40--60%; well-optimized comparison sites target < 45% (source: SEMrush benchmarks).

### 2.3 Pages Per Session

| Period | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Month 3 | > 2.0 |
| Month 6 | > 3.0 |
| Month 12 | > 3.5 |

**Benchmark:** E-commerce average is 2.0--2.5 pages/session; Netflix-style horizontal browsing UX is designed to increase this by encouraging lateral category exploration.

### 2.4 Return Visitor Rate

| Period | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Month 3 | > 15% | Early adopters beginning to return |
| Month 6 | > 30% | Newsletter, bookmarks, and seasonal content drive returns |
| Month 12 | > 35% | Established habit among foreign resident community |

**Benchmark:** Content sites average 25--30% return visitors; community-oriented sites can reach 40%+ (source: industry averages).

## 3. Monetisation KPIs

### 3.1 Affiliate Click-Through Rate (CTR)

| Period | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Month 3 | 2.0% | Initial CTR as CTA placement is tested |
| Month 6 | 3.0% | Optimised button placement, trust established |
| Month 12 | 4.0% | A/B tested CTAs, strong editorial trust |

**Benchmark:** Affiliate CTR benchmarks range from 2--5% for well-optimised content sites (source: Lasso affiliate marketing benchmarks). mybest and Wirecutter-style "Best Pick" formats with prominent buy buttons typically achieve 3--5%.

### 3.2 Monthly Affiliate Revenue (JPY)

| Period | Target | Calculation basis |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Month 3 | JPY 15,000 | 5,000 visitors x 2% CTR x 50% conversion x JPY 5,000 AOV x 4% commission = JPY 10,000--20,000 |
| Month 6 | JPY 75,000 | 15,000 visitors x 3% CTR x 50% conversion x JPY 5,000 AOV x 4% commission |
| Month 12 | JPY 150,000 | 50,000 visitors x 3.5% CTR x 50% conversion x JPY 5,000 AOV x 4% commission |

**Note:** "50% conversion" refers to the estimated percentage of affiliate clicks that result in a completed purchase within the cookie window. Amazon Japan's cookie window is 24 hours. Commission rates based on Amazon Associates (2--8%) and Rakuten (3--7%) blended average of ~4%.

### 3.3 Revenue Per Visitor (RPV)

| Period | Target (JPY) |
|--------|--------------|
| Month 6 | JPY 5.0 |
| Month 12 | JPY 3.0 |

**Note:** RPV may decrease as traffic scales because newer visitors have lower purchase intent. This is expected and healthy if absolute revenue grows.

## 5. Technical Performance KPIs

| Metric | Target | Source |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) | < 2.5 seconds | Google Core Web Vitals "Good" threshold |
| First Input Delay (FID) / Interaction to Next Paint (INP) | < 100ms / < 200ms | Google Core Web Vitals |
| Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) | < 0.1 | Google Core Web Vitals |
| Page weight (initial load, compressed) | < 500 KB | Performance budget |
| Time to First Byte (TTFB) | < 800ms | Google recommendation |
| Lighthouse Performance score | >= 90 | Google Lighthouse |
| Uptime | 99.9% | CDN SLA (Cloudflare/Vercel) |

## 7. KPI Tracking & Review Cadence

| Frequency | Activity |
|-----------|----------|
| Daily | Check Google Search Console for crawl errors; monitor uptime |
| Weekly | Review Google Analytics traffic, engagement metrics, top pages |
| Bi-weekly | Review affiliate dashboard revenue, CTR, conversion |
| Monthly | Full KPI review against targets; adjust content strategy |
| Quarterly | Strategic review; update projections; competitor benchmarking |
