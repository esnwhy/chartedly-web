## Price Legend & Shopping Tips

### Average Price Ranges
- **Budget snacks**: 30-200 JPY (umaibo, onigiri, small chocolate bars)
- **Standard snacks/drinks**: 150-350 JPY (chips, bottled drinks, canned coffee)
- **Premium items**: 300-800 JPY (specialty desserts, craft drinks, small gift packs)
- **Souvenir packs**: 500-2,000 JPY (variety packs, gift boxes, beauty products)
- **Donki specials**: 300-10,000 JPY (wide range from cheap gadgets to quality knives)

### Tax-Free Shopping
- Don Quijote offers tax-free shopping for purchases over 5,000 JPY (excluding tax)
- Download the "Majica" app or get the coupon at the tourist counter for additional 5% off
- Konbini do NOT offer tax-free shopping

### Best Stores by Category
- **Fried chicken**: FamilyMart (Famichiki) > Lawson (Karaage-kun) > 7-Eleven (Nana Chiki)
- **Desserts & sweets**: Lawson (Uchi Cafe) > FamilyMart > 7-Eleven
- **Onigiri & sandwiches**: 7-Eleven > FamilyMart > Lawson
- **Coffee**: 7-Eleven (Seven Cafe) > Lawson (Machi Cafe) > FamilyMart
- **Beauty products**: Don Quijote (widest selection, tax-free, cheapest)
- **Bulk souvenirs**: Don Quijote (variety packs, bulk pricing, tax-free)
- **Anime/character goods**: Don Quijote + Lawson (anime collabs)

### Seasonal Must-Buys
- **Spring (Mar-May)**: Sakura KitKats, cherry blossom drinks, sakura mochi
- **Summer (Jun-Aug)**: Ramune, melon soda, portable fans (Donki), ice cream varieties
- **Autumn (Sep-Nov)**: Sweet potato KitKats, chestnut sweets, pumpkin drinks
- **Winter (Dec-Feb)**: Nikuman (meat buns), oden, hot canned coffee, chocolate varieties
