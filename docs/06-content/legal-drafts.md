## 1. Affiliate Disclosure

### Full Page -- English

**Page Title:** Affiliate Disclosure

**URL:** /affiliate-disclosure

Chartedly is an independent product comparison website. We earn money through affiliate partnerships, primarily with Amazon Japan (Amazon.co.jp) through the Amazon Associates Program.

**What this means for you:**

When you click a product link on Chartedly and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. The price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer.

**What this means for our recommendations:**

Our editorial picks are made independently, before affiliate links are added. We choose products based on research, testing, and documented scoring criteria -- never based on which product pays the highest commission.

If our top pick has no affiliate program, we still recommend it. We will link to a non-affiliate purchase option and note it clearly.

We will never:
- Recommend a product because it pays a higher commission
- Hide the fact that a link is an affiliate link
- Rank a product higher because of its affiliate rate
- Omit a better product that lacks an affiliate program

**How much do we earn?**

Amazon Associates commissions in Japan typically range from 2% to 10% of the purchase price, depending on the product category. For a 900-yen sunscreen, we might earn 18 to 90 yen (roughly $0.12 to $0.60 USD). These small amounts add up to help us keep the site running.

**Other potential revenue:**

In the future, Chartedly may earn revenue through display advertising and sponsored content. Sponsored content will always be clearly labeled with a "Sponsored" badge and will be separated from our independent editorial comparisons.

**Questions?**

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships or how we make money, email us at hello@chartedly.com. We believe in full transparency.

Last updated: March 2026

## 2. Privacy Policy

### English

**Page Title:** Privacy Policy

**URL:** /privacy-policy

**Last Updated:** March 24, 2026

**1. Who We Are**

Chartedly ("we," "us," "our") operates the website chartedly.com. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Contact: hello@chartedly.com

**2. Information We Collect**

*Information you provide directly:*
- Email address (if you subscribe to our newsletter)
- Name and message content (if you use our contact form)

*Information collected automatically:*
- IP address (anonymized where required by law)
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referring website
- Approximate geographic location (country/region level only)

*Cookies and similar technologies:*
- Essential cookies for site functionality
- Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4) for understanding how visitors use our site
- Affiliate tracking cookies (Amazon Associates) to attribute purchases made through our links

**3. How We Use Your Information**

We use collected information to:
- Deliver and improve the Chartedly website
- Send our newsletter to subscribers who have opted in
- Respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form
- Analyze site traffic and user behavior to improve content
- Track affiliate commissions through partner programs
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

**4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)**

For visitors in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process personal data based on:
- **Consent:** Newsletter subscription, non-essential cookies
- **Legitimate interest:** Analytics to improve our website, affiliate link tracking
- **Legal obligation:** Where required by applicable law

**5. Third-Party Services**

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

| Service | Purpose | Data Collected | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|---------------|
| Google Analytics 4 | Site analytics | Anonymized usage data | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Amazon Associates | Affiliate tracking | Purchase attribution | [Amazon Privacy Policy](https://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=GX7NJQ4ZB8MHFRNJ) |
| Cloudflare | CDN and security | IP address, request data | [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) |
| Mailchimp/Buttondown | Newsletter delivery | Email address | See provider's privacy policy |

**6. Cookies**

See our Cookie Notice (Section 4 of this document) for detailed information about the cookies we use.

**7. Your Rights**

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent at any time

**For EEA residents (GDPR):** You have all rights listed above. Contact us at hello@chartedly.com to exercise them. We will respond within 30 days.

**For Japan residents (APPI -- 個人情報保護法):** Under the Act on the Protection of Personal Information, you have the right to request disclosure, correction, cessation of use, or deletion of your personal information. Contact us at hello@chartedly.com.

**For California residents (CCPA):** You have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

**8. Data Retention**

- Newsletter subscriber data: Retained until you unsubscribe
- Analytics data: Retained for 14 months (Google Analytics default)
- Contact form messages: Retained for 12 months, then deleted
- Server logs: Retained for 30 days

**9. Data Security**

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and secure hosting through Cloudflare. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your data.

**10. Children's Privacy**

Chartedly is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will delete it promptly.

**11. International Data Transfers**

Our servers are located in multiple regions through Cloudflare's global network. If you are located outside of Japan, your data may be transferred to and processed in Japan or other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

**12. Changes to This Policy**

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision. Significant changes will be communicated via a notice on our website.

**13. Contact Us**

For privacy-related inquiries:
- Email: hello@chartedly.com
- Response time: Within 30 days
